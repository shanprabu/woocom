Quick Sales WordPress Theme
---------------------------------

Quick Sales Theme, Copyright 2015 OptArt
Quick Sales is distributed under the terms of the GNU GPL

Quick Sales is a basic and elegant WooCommerce theme which is made to help you sell your products quickly. The theme, which is very clean and smooth, stands out with large product pages which will surely attract clients if you attach high resolution images to your products. The theme is white, orange and grey by default, but its colors can be customized freely.

Installation
---------------------------------

- Connect to your server’s FTP
- Copy the theme folder from the zip into the “wp-content/themes” directory
- Login to your WordPress administrator panel
- Activate the theme in Appearance -> Themes


Usage
---------------------------------

- The theme can be easily customized by the administrator panel -> Appearance -> Customize.
- We suggest setting product page as front page.
